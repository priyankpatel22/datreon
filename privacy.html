Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

Datreon respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you interact with us, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Who We Are

Datreon is a software engineering and AI consulting firm based in Chesapeake, Virginia, United States. For any privacy-related questions, contact us at hello@datreon.ai.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect information that helps us respond to you and run our business:

  • Contact details you provide directly — such as your name, email address, company, and the contents of any message or inquiry you send us.
  • Project information you share during scoping, proposals, or engagements.
  • Basic technical data your browser sends automatically when you visit this site, such as IP address, device and browser type, and pages viewed. This is used only for security and to understand aggregate traffic.

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information, and we do not collect information from children under 13.

3. How We Use Information

  • To respond to inquiries and communicate with you about a potential or active engagement.
  • To deliver, support, and improve our services.
  • To meet legal, contractual, and accounting obligations.
  • To protect the security and integrity of our systems.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for third-party advertising.

4. How We Share Information

We share information only when necessary:

  • With trusted service providers (for example, email, hosting, and analytics vendors) who process data on our behalf under confidentiality obligations.
  • When required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Datreon, our clients, or others.

5. Data Retention

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes described here, to maintain business records, or as required by law. When it is no longer needed, we delete or anonymize it.

6. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal information, or to object to certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, email hello@datreon.ai and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to safeguard the data entrusted to us.

8. International Visitors

Datreon is based in the United States and serves clients internationally. If you contact us from outside the U.S., your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Material changes will be communicated where appropriate.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Reach us at hello@datreon.ai.
